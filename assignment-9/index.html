Making S'mores

This recipe will help you make eight s'mores right in the comfort of your own home!

Required Materials

  • Cooking supplies
    • An oven
    • A baking sheet
    • Parchment baking paper
  • Ingredients
    • 8 marshmallows
    • 16 graham cracker squares
    • A bar of chocolate (milk or dark)

Where to Find Materials

Walmart is avaliable in most places in the US, and will likely have all you need to make s'mores.

Instructions

  1. First, preheat the oven to 400 °F
  2. Next, cut or rip a rectangle of parchment baking paper to cover the bottom of your baking sheet
  3. Then, place 8 graham cracker squares spaced evenly on the baking sheet
  4. Put a marshmallow on each graham cracker square
  5. Place the baking sheet into the oven on the rack closest to the top of the oven
  6. Wait 3-5 minutes for the marshmallows to bake, or until they are puffed up and golden brown
  7. Take the baking sheet out of the oven, and quickly place a piece of chocolate on each marshmallow
  8. Use a square of graham cracker on top to finish assembling the s'mores
  9. Enjoy!
