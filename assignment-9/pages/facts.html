Fun S'more Facts

  • Why are s'mores called s'mores?
    • S'more is a contraction of the phrase "some more."
  • What is the origin of the s'more?
    • S'mores first appeared in a cookbook in the 1920s, but the recipe said it was already a popular treat with Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts.
  • What is the origin of marshmallows?
    • Marshmallows used to be medicinal, named after the herb, "mallow root," it was used to soothe coughs and sore throats.
  • Who inspired the invention of graham crackers?
    • Sylvester Graham, who believed a godly lifestyle included a vegetarian diet featuring lots of wheat, and minimizing pleasure and stimulation.
